Omelette's Recipe

Description:

Classic folded turkey omelette using leftover roasted turkey and savory seasoning.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh shiitake mushroom
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 fresh sage leaf, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup shredded cooked turkey
  • 1 teaspoon dry vermouth (Optional)
  • 2 eggs, chilled
  • 1 pinch ground white pepper
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon creme fraiche

Steps:

  1. Heat oil and butter in a skillet. Cook until shallot is translucent.
  2. Add mushroom, shallot, salt, sage, and red pepper flakes.
  3. Add turkey and vermouth; stir until heated through. Remove from heat.
  4. Beat eggs in a bowl, season with salt and white pepper.
  5. Heat oil in a pan. Pour beaten eggs and gently stir until partially set.

  7. Transfer omelette onto a plate.
